About

Inom Mirzaev

I am an Applied Mathematician with experience in Data Science, Numerical Optimization and Simulation of PDEs. I love solving problems by coding. I have good experience in training machine learning and GPU accelerated deep learning algorithms. I received my B.S. from Orta Dogu Teknik Universitesi in Ankara, Turkey and my Ph.D. from the University of Colorado Boulder. I currently have a joint postdoctoral position with the Mathematical Biosciences Institute and the Cleveland Clinic. When I am not coding and doing math, I like to bike, play basketball/soccer and watch soccer games.
